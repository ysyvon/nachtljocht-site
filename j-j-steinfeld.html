J. J. Steinfeld

Canadian fiction writer, poet, and playwright J. J. Steinfeld lives on Prince Edward Island, where he is patiently waiting for Godot’s arrival and a phone call from Kafka. While waiting, he has published 25 books, including An Unauthorized Biography of Being, A Visit to the Kafka Café, Gregor Samsa Was Never in The Beatles, Somewhat Absurd, Somehow Existential, Acting on the Island, As You Continue to Wait, and My Post-Holocaust Second Generation Voice: History/Memory/Identity; over 60 of his one-act plays and a handful of full-length plays have been performed in Canada and the United States.
