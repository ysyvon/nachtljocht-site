Zoe Carver

Zoe Carver is an emerging writer with works in the New English Review, Foofaraw Press, and Ouch Collective. She is originally from Portland, Oregon, and studied International Affairs and Creative Writing at George Washington University. She was awarded the Julian Clement Chase Writing in Washington Award, and is currently on fellowship at the American University in Cairo in Cairo, Egypt.
